Welcome to Heart Rate Monitor

How It Works

The Heart Rate Monitor is designed to help you easily track and monitor your heart health. Here's how it works:

  • Pulse Sensor: The integrated pulse sensor ensures accurate heart rate monitoring by measuring the pulse rate from your fingertips.
  • Website Interface: The user-friendly website interface provides real-time heart rate readings, accessible from any device with internet connectivity.
  • Database Connectivity: Secure database connectivity allows multiple users to access and monitor heart rate data simultaneously, ensuring efficient and convenient tracking.
  • Companion Mobile App: The companion mobile app, specifically designed for elderly users, provides easy access to heart rate readings on-the-go, ensuring continuous monitoring and timely intervention when needed.

Parts

  • Pulse Sensor

  • ESP8266 Wi-Fi Module

  • Database Connectivity

  • Companion Mobile App

For Doctors:

  • Real-Time Heart Rate Monitoring: Doctors can monitor patients' heart rates in real-time, allowing for immediate intervention if necessary.
  • Data Analysis Tools: Access to comprehensive heart rate data enables doctors to analyze trends, identify patterns, and make informed decisions about patient care.
  • Customizable Alerts: Doctors can set custom alerts to receive notifications when patients' heart rates fall outside normal ranges, facilitating timely intervention.
  • Secure Access: The system ensures that only authorized medical personnel can access patient data, maintaining patient confidentiality and complying with privacy regulations.

For Patients:

  • User-Friendly Interface: Patients can easily monitor their own heart rates using a simple and intuitive mobile app interface.
  • Visualized Data: Clear visualization of heart rate data helps patients understand their heart health status and track changes over time.
  • Alert Notifications: Patients receive notifications on their mobile devices if their heart rate exceeds or falls below safe levels, prompting them to take appropriate actions.
  • Data Privacy: Patients can trust that their heart rate data is securely stored and accessible only to authorized medical professionals, ensuring their privacy is protected.

For Nurses:

  • Remote Monitoring: Nurses can remotely monitor multiple patients' heart rates simultaneously, allowing them to prioritize care based on urgency.
  • Efficient Workflow: Integration with patient management systems streamlines workflow, enabling nurses to access heart rate data alongside other patient information.
  • Customizable Settings: Nurses can customize alert thresholds and notification preferences to align with individual patient needs and care plans.
  • Collaborative Care: Nurses can easily share heart rate data with doctors and other healthcare providers, facilitating collaborative decision-making and coordinated patient care.
